How to reach Taki Goverment High School from Kolkata

  1. Take a train towards Hasnabad from Sealdah or Dumdum.
  2. Disembark at Taki Road station. It's the penultimate station of the Hasnabad line.
  3. Walk eastwards via Taki Ghat Road for about 20 minutes or take a van-rickshaw to reach the school.
